## What You'll Do

Follow these steps to build and deploy your own OP Stack chain:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build your chain">
    Set up your own OP Stack testnet chain using our deployment tools and configuration guides.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure your chain">
    Set RPC endpoints, chain parameters, and analytics to customize your chain's behavior.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Test your chain">
    Use block explorers and send test transactions to validate your chain's functionality.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Launch on mainnet">
    Complete setup and join the Superchain network with your production-ready chain.
  </Step>
</Steps>
